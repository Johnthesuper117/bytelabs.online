An informative documant on the Bushido Code


Bushidō (武士道 "the way of the warrior") was a code of conduct for the samurai class and has multiple variations that differ slightly between clans, but they usually include these 8 virtues:

  • 義 Honor: A samurai's most important virtue, encompassing personal integrity and a strong sense of duty.
  • 忠 Loyalty: Unwavering devotion to one's lord, family, and country.
  • 勇 Courage: Bravery in the face of danger and adversity, not just physical but also moral.
  • 自律 Self-Discipline: Strict adherence to rules and principles, both in training and in life.
  • 恭 Respect: Courtesy and politeness towards others, especially those of higher status.
  • 直 Honesty: Truthfulness and integrity in all dealings.
  • 徳 Benevolence: Compassion and kindness towards others.
  • 理 Justice: Acting with justice and righteousness.
